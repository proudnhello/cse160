A scuffed version of E1M1 of Wolfenstein 3D, created with webgl.
WASD to move, Q and E or click dragging the mouse to rotate the camera
Press space to destory the enemy (or wall, if you're feeling unsportmanlike) in front of you
Press 1-8 to place down walls in front of you, or make the stack of walls in front of you one higher
